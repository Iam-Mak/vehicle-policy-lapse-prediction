# Business Context

## Why Policy Lapse Prediction Matters

Policy lapses directly impact recurring premium revenue and long-term customer value. Retaining existing customers is significantly more cost-effective than acquiring new ones, making early identification of at-risk policies important for insurers.

A predictive system enables:

* Early detection of high-risk policies
* Targeted retention strategies
* Improved revenue stability and forecasting
* More efficient allocation of marketing and engagement efforts

## Business Challenges

Policy lapse prediction is non-trivial due to several factors:

* Lapses often occur passively (missed renewals), making intent difficult to observe
* Clear signals typically appear close to renewal, limiting intervention time
* Customer sensitivity to pricing varies across segments
* Key drivers (e.g., financial conditions, usage changes) are not directly observable
* Revenue impact is uneven across policies, requiring prioritization

## Solution Approach

The system uses historical policy, customer, and vehicle data to estimate the probability of lapse.

The output is a probability score that can be used to:

* Rank customers by risk level
* Prioritize retention efforts
* Support proactive engagement strategies

The approach focuses on producing interpretable and operationally usable predictions that can integrate into downstream business workflows.

## Summary

This system provides a data-driven approach to identifying policy lapse risk, supporting retention strategies and reducing revenue loss through earlier intervention.
